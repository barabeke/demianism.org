Privacy Policy

Demian — The Demianist AI · demianism.org · Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how Demian handles your data. It is written to be honest and readable, not to obscure important things in legal language.

Demian is a global service operated from the EU by Barabeke, an independent Italian creator and artist. As a service operated from within the European Union, it is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Who you are talking to

Demian is an AI. It is not a human. This is stated clearly at the beginning of every conversation and is fundamental to how Demianism understands AI: a technological tool, not a conscious being.

The AI is powered by the Claude API, provided by Anthropic, PBC (San Francisco, USA). Anthropic acts as a data processor on behalf of Barabeke (the data controller) under a Data Processing Agreement that includes EU Standard Contractual Clauses for international data transfers.

What data is collected and why

Conversation content. Your messages and Demian's responses are processed by Anthropic's servers to generate replies. Anthropic's API policy does not use these conversations to train AI models. Conversation data is retained by Anthropic for up to 7 days for abuse prevention, then deleted.

Anonymous session logs. Demian keeps anonymous logs of conversations — identified only by a random session ID, never by your name, email, or IP address. These logs are retained for up to 12 months for two purposes: improving the service and maintaining a record in case of safety or legal concerns. They cannot be traced back to you under normal circumstances.

Email address. Only collected if you choose to purchase credits or a mantra session. Your email is stored separately from conversation logs and is used only to manage your account and deliver what you purchased. If you explicitly opt in, it may also be used to send you occasional updates via The Odd Gospel, Barabeke's Substack newsletter — you can unsubscribe at any time. Your email is never sold or shared with third parties. It can be deleted on request.

IP address. Used temporarily for rate limiting and session management. Not stored permanently. Not linked to conversation content in logs.

Payment data. Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. Barabeke never sees or stores your card details. Stripe's own privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6)

Legitimate interest — anonymous conversation logs for safety monitoring and service improvement; rate limiting; abuse prevention.

Contract performance — processing your email and payment data to deliver the service you purchased.

Consent — if you choose to share personal information that would make you identifiable during a conversation (Demian never asks for it), that is your own choice. Consent is also the basis for any optional email communications.

International data transfers

Anthropic is a US company. Your conversation content is processed on servers in the United States. This transfer is covered by EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under Anthropic's Data Processing Agreement, which complies with GDPR requirements for international transfers.

Cloudflare (session management and anonymous logs) operates a global network including EU data centers.

What Demian does not collect

Demian does not ask for your real name, age, or location. It does not use tracking cookies. It does not build profiles for advertising. It does not share data with advertisers or marketing platforms.

Safety

Demian operates two layers of safety protection. The first is Anthropic's Constitutional AI framework — a robust automated system built into the underlying model that handles explicit self-harm signals, clinical crises, and other high-risk content before a response is generated.

The second layer is Demian's own logic: if a conversation contains language associated with personal crisis, it is flagged for potential human review. Flagged conversations remain anonymous unless a serious legal concern requires identifying the source.

Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, reach out via X (@barabeke) or Telegram.

Because conversations are logged anonymously, fulfilling access or erasure requests for conversation content requires you to provide your session ID (visible in your browser's developer tools under network requests to the Demian backend). Without it, individual conversations cannot be identified.

If you are located in the EU, you may also lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

Data retention

Anonymous conversation logs: 12 months, then automatically deleted.
Email addresses (paying users): retained while the account is active, deleted within 30 days of a deletion request.
Payment records: retained as required by applicable tax law, stored by Stripe.

Age

Demian is not intended for children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this service.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the service evolves. The date at the top reflects the last update. Continued use of Demian after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact
